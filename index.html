To nurture the blooming of a new mother.

“It takes a village to raise a child”

Yoruba Proverb

In a world where support often feels scarce, remember: you were never meant to do this alone. In ancient traditions, the art of "spoiling" new mothers was cherished for its profound impact on the well-being of both mother and child, benefiting the entire community. Yet, in today's fast-paced society, this nurturing practice has faded.

Tell me more...

As your postpartum doula, I step into this ancient role, offering you the care and celebration you deserve after birthing your baby. Picture our first visit: I may pamper you with a soothing foot soak, listening intently as we craft a plan for our session. We'll chat about baby bath techniques, and I'll whip up a nutritious snack for those endless nights. I'll happily tackle errands, fold baby clothes with care, and introduce you to self-care practices like postnatal yoga, self-massage, and calming breathing exercises—tailored just for you.

Every session is a personalized journey, designed to nurture and support you and your family. It's a blessing!

Yoga

Designed for new moms, this class offers a restorative space to release and restore, all while strengthening and preparing your body for the journey ahead. We infuse elements of dance into our practice to make it not just beneficial but also exciting and fun! Our classes are tailored to the specific needs of expectant and new mothers, emphasizing holistic health through somatic movements, meditation, and mindfulness. Join us and experience the joy of prenatal yoga!

The first half focuses on core and pelvic muscles with a gentle vinyasa, perfect for beginners and postpartum moms. The second half includes restorative poses for deep relaxation, with variations to suit all levels. Whether you're new to yoga or experienced, this class is designed to support your recovery from childbirth, build strength, and reduce stress.

Mother Blessing Ceremony

A mother's blessing is a heartfelt gathering inspired by the ancient Navajo ceremony known as a Blessing Way. This special event is a beautiful way to prepare an expectant mother for her upcoming birth journey, surrounding her with love, support, and encouragement.

In many cultures, birth is viewed as a powerful rite of passage for women, and a mother's blessing is designed to help a woman feel mentally, physically, and emotionally ready for childbirth. While honoring the traditions of the Navajo blessing way, a Mother's Blessing offers a modern twist, providing moms-to-be with a similar sense of support and honor during this sacred time.

Meet us

Jacque
Founder, Movement Teacher, Doula

Jacque is a vibrant Afro-Indigenous woman hailing from Brazil! Originally studying Social Sciences, her path took a twist when she discovered a passion for body movement and healing. Her journey led her to Medellin, Colombia, where in 2015, she began sharing the gift of prenatal yoga. Fascinated by natural health methods, Jacque immersed herself in ancestral wisdom, honoring her lineage. As the founder of Doula Mami Moon, Jacque is deeply committed to serving the DMV community. Her unique blend of traditional knowledge and modern practices brings a holistic approach to wellness.

A good listener, calm and creative problem solving. Culturally informed, she speaks 3 languages: Portuguese, Spanish and English. Jacque is a kind and warm professional so expect to be respected, supported and educated through your postpartum journey.

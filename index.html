Who mothers the mother?

As your postpartum doula, I’m here to nurture and care for you in the time after you birth your baby.

On my first visit I may prepare you a foot soak as we go through how you are feeling and outline a plan for that session. We may talk about ways of bathing the baby as I prepare a nutritious snack for those timeless nights when your baby has all the energy in the world. I can run some errands for you, fold all your baby clothes, and also share some self care practices like postnatal yoga, self massage or breathing exercise to help you release tension and nurture yourself. I am here to support you and your family. All sessions are designed in a personalized and unique way. It’s a blessing!

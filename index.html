To nurture the blooming of a new mother.

“It takes a village to raise a child”

Yoruba Proverb

Remember, we were never meant to do this alone. In ancestral societies, the tradition of “spoiling” the new mother after childbirth is recognized as essential for the holistic well-being of both the mother and her baby (so the whole society). Sadly, in today's western culture with diminishing communal ties, it appears as though we have lost the skill of nurturing the Mother.

As your postpartum doula, I’m here to nurture and care for you so you can feel supported and celebrated in the time after you birth your baby.

Tell me more...

On my first visit I may prepare you a foot soak as we go through how you are feeling and outline a plan for that session. We may talk about ways of bathing the baby as I prepare a nutritious snack for those timeless nights when your baby has all the energy in the world! I can run some errands for you, fold all your baby clothes, and also share some self-care practices like postnatal yoga, self massage or breathing exercise to help you release tension and nurture yourself. All sessions are designed in a personalized and unique way. I am here to support you and your family. It's a blessing!

Meet me

Born and raised in Brazil, Jacque was studying Social Sciences when she became interested in body movement and healing. Services

Mami Moon is more than just a collection of services; it's a nurturing and compassionate approach to postpartum care. By addressing the physical, emotional, and social needs of new mothers, we aim to empower them to embrace their journey into motherhood with confidence and positivity.

All my postpartum packages are centered around a series of visits in your home that typically last four hours at a time.

At the start of each visit, we’ll sit down together to discuss how you’re feeling and how I can best help you feel supported and nurtured that day. This may include any of the following:

Emotional support
  • Non-judgemental emotional support;
  • Discussing how to increase oxytocin and identifying what brings you ‘peace and joy’;
  • Gentle and comforting massage;
  • Treating you to a foot soak;
  • Goal and intention setting;
  • Helping to identify and establish boundaries;
  • Helping communications with your partner and family members;
  • Identifying potential issues and working with you to problem solve;
  • Attending appointments with you if you’d like a bit of extra support.
